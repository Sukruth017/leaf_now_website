Sedum adolphii ‘Firestorm’

Sedum adolphii ‘Firestorm’is a stunning form of Golden Sedum featuring orange to fiery red margins. Displays pretty, white, star-shaped flowers.

A small, slow-growing, low maintenance plant. Provide a well-drained soil away from reflected heat. Once established, water infrequently during the hot season. Ideal in warm climate landscapes and container gardens. Firestorm is especially happy sitting on your windowsill as a houseplant. Protect your PRINCE OF PARADISE™ from frost!
















Echeveria ‘Ponto Rosa’

Native to Mexico, this succulent features rosettes with spoon-shaped, blue-green leaves and bright pink tips.


Echeveria are low maintenance, and require little water once established. Provide a well-drained soil away from reflected heat. Ideal in warm climate landscapes and container gardens, but is especially happy sitting on your windowsill as a houseplant. Protect your PRINCE OF PARADISE™ from frost!



Colors (Foliage): Blue/Blue-green, Pink
Growth Habit: Low
Recommended Light Conditions: Bright Indoor Light, Filtered/Partial Sun, Full Sun, Partial Shade
Location: Exterior, Interior

ARECA PALM PLANT

Dypsis lutescens also known as golden cane palm,areca palm, yellow palm ,or butterfly palm is a species of flowering plant in the family of Arecaceae,native to Madagascar and naturalized in the Andaman Island.It grows upto 6-12m(20-39ft) in height.

Once an endangered species, the Areca Palm is a staple in Indian spaces. One of the most undervalued plants in the indoor plant category. This excellent air purifier is pet friendly and easy to grow. Its delicate leaves/fronds grow outwards in a curved manner and add a tropical feel to any space and can adapt to a variety of growing conditions.



SUNLIGHT: Areca palms require bright ,indirect light,but direct sunlight may burn the leaves.
WATER :Water them often enough to keep the soil little moist in spring and summer, and allow the soil to dry slightly between waterings in fall and winter.
TEMPERATURE: Home temperature between 60°-75°F.
